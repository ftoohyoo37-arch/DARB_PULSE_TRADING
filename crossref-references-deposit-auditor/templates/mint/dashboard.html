DOI minting

Workflow for journals that publish whole-issue PDFs and don't yet have CrossRef DOIs. Split issue PDFs into per-article PDFs, capture table-of-contents metadata, and generate a CrossRef content-registration deposit XML that mints a DOI for each article.

To participate, a journal needs an issue_pdfs/ subdirectory containing whole-issue PDFs (one per issue), plus a DOI_PREFIX setting in its journal.py.
